Dieter Rams

10 Principles for Good Design

1 Good design is innovative

2 Good design makes a product useful

3 Good design is aesthetic

4 Good design is understandable

5 Good design is unobtrusive

6 Good design is honest

7 Good design is long-lasting

8 Good design is attention to detail

9 Good design is environmentally friendly

10 Good design is as little design as possible

1

Good design is innovative

There is always something that hasn’t been done before.

2

Good design makes a product useful

Useability is important, unnecessary design can make a product difficult to use.

3

Good design is aesthetic

The way a product looks contributes to its usefulness because we use them everyday and if affects us.

4

Good design makes a product understandable

The use of the product should be obvious and not confusing to the user.

5

Good design is unobtrusive

It’s not about the designer who designed the product, the user should be able to use the product for self-expression.

6

Good design is honest

The goal isn’t to manipulate a user, but to be upfront about the product.

7

Good design is long-lasting

Unlike fashion where trends get old quick, the design sticks around for years.

8

Good design is thorough down to the last detail

The more careful and accurate you are with a design, shows respect towards the consumer.

9

Good design is environmentally friendly

It’s important to contribute to the preservation of the environment.

10

Good design is as little design as possible

Don’t burden products with non-essentials, keep it simple. Less but better.
